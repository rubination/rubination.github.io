About Me

My name is Jake Rubin and I'm a computer engineering student from Vancouver, Canada. I am entering my third year of the University of British Columbia's computer engineering program, expecting to graduate in April 2024.

My interest in programming began at age 11 when my uncle introduced me to Codecademy, and I first experimented with rudimentary HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I then took information technology courses in high school which further reinforced my passion.

I'm interested to learn how to work in information technology, web development, machine learning and augmented reality. I'm learning every day, and am excited to gain more knowledge and experience.
